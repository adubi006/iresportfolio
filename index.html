South African Language Identification

Natural language programming (NLP)

August 18, 2023 IreOluwa Ige

South Africa is a multicultural society that is characterised by its rich linguistic diversity. Language is an indispensable tool that can be used to deepen democracy and also contribute to the social, cultural, intellectual, economic and political life of the South African society. The country is multilingual with 11 official languages, each of which is guaranteed equal status. Most South Africans are multilingual and able to speak at least two or more of the official languages. With such a multilingual population, it is only obvious that the systems and devices also communicate in multi-languages. In this project, we take text which is in any of South Africa's 11 Official languages and identify which language the text is in.

ESKOM: Infrastructure, Capacity & Efficiency

Finding opportunities for energy optimization

February 28, 2023 IreOluwa Ige

South Africa is experiencing regular power cuts referred to as "load-shedding" and it is unable to meet the energy demands of the country. The energy crisis in South Africa is particularly apparent in the frequency of load shedding: a technique of deliberately inducing rolling blackouts nationwide in order to prevent a destabilization and wipeout of the entire national grid. Power is shut off for up to seven hours a day almost every single day. In this project, I create three dashboards to report the infrastructure, capacity & efficiency, and Twitter sentiments of ESKOM Energy.
