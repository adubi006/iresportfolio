Background

The client is an educational institution established with the mission of imparting data and AI skills to the upcoming generation. Recognizing the fluid and evolving nature of education, the client faced a sobering truth: their marketing endeavors yielded subpar results due to a lack of comprehensive understanding about their students. This realization highlighted the imperative for the client to construct a student 'persona' database encompassing its diverse student body. This involves gathering and analyzing student data pertaining to their backgrounds and overall experiences, aimed at enhancing both content delivery and marketing strategies.

Scope

The main aim of this project is to develop an interactive Discord Bot for a student Discord server. The primary purpose of the bot is to cater for student engagement on a weekly basis, by asking students some listed well-structured questions from a Google Doc /Google Spreadsheet.  The bot sends 2-3 questions on a weekly basis sourcing data from Google documents, responses will be recorded and stored in a Google Doc/Google Spreadsheet. The bot should be fun, and frictionless i.e. should be gamified. Furthermore, the collected data will need to be analysed and visualized on  Power BI and Looker Studio to provide further insights and aid decision-making.

Other requirements include: - Configure GCP to be able to use Google APIS to store data in Google Sheets. - Creating a data ETL pipeline that will be able to transfer the data into the Google spreadsheets and read data from the Google drive.
